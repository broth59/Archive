### 설정값 

```sql
SELECT B.CONFIG_NO,B.BSNS_CODE, B.PROPERTY_NO, B.CONFIG_CODE, A.CONFIG_ID, B.NAME_OF_VALUE, B.VALUE_CHR_1, B.VALUE_CHR_2
       FROM TB_ZZ_CONFIG_APP_DTL B, TB_ZZ_CONFIG_APP_MST A
      WHERE A.CONFIG_NO = B.CONFIG_NO
        AND A.APPLICATION_ID = 'POLICY_OF_RSVN_FOR_MEMBER'
        AND A.CONFIG_ID			= 'MEMBER_RSVN_RESTRICTION_CHECK_YN'
        AND B.BSNS_CODE = '81'
        AND B.PROPERTY_NO = '81'
        AND B.DEL_YN = 'N'
```
